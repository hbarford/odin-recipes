Crockpot Salsa Chicken




Slow Cooker Taco Chicken Bowls are the ultimate "set it and forget it" easy weeknight meal that the whole family will love.



Ingredients:


  • 1 1/2 lbs. chicken breasts
  • 1 16 oz. jar salsa
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (more to taste)
  • 1 15 oz. can black beans
  • 1/2 lb frozen corn
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground oregano
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (more to taste)
  • freshly cracked black pepper
  • 6 cups cooked rice
  • 8 oz. shredded cheddar or mexican blend cheese
  • 3 green onions, sliced (or fresh cilantro)
